Interview

An applicant for a job in a very prestigious company will be interviewed only if his/her age range is between 25 and 40 inclusive, or the applicant has ten years of experience and he/she is not older than forty five.

For this application you have to set values for age and experience, and expected values for interview.
    age and experience should be whole numbers
    interview should be true or false.

Notes:
1: There may be no errors to find.
2: Description and coverage are important.
3: Avoid numbers in your description
4: Do not write more than 10 tests.
