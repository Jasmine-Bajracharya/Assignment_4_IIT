Referendum

To pass a referendum, a motion has to get more than half of the votes and more than half of the states.
A state is in favour if more than half of the voters are in favour.

For this application you have to set values for stateVotesFor and stateVotesAgainst, and expected values for outcome().
    stateVotesFor and stateVotesAgainst should be comma separated lists of whole numbers. Note that outcome() refers to passing or not the referendum.
    The two lists should be the same length.

    outcome() should be Y or N.

Do not test for invalid data!!1

 Notes:
1: There may be no errors to find.
2: Description and coverage are important.
3: Avoid numbers in your description
4: Do not write more than 10 tests. You will not need that many.
